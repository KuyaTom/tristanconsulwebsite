Tristan's Personality


Tristan appears to most as a very mischievious, ambitious, creative, and easy-going.


He makes a lot of jokes and is usually seen acting very playful and childish. He acts this way now as he used to be very shy and introverted. But, there is a lot more to him than meets the eye.

Mischievous / Ambitious / Creative / Easy-Going

Mischievous / Jokey

Tristan often enjoys trolling his friends and sometimes his teachers. He acts very foolish and playful, but he can be serious if he chooses to or if absolutely necessary. More often than not he is seen cracking jokes and spitting out nonsense.

Ambitious


This word literally means too much or to be extra. When faced with a task that he is dedicated to doing, Tristan will come up with the most ambitious plans prior to actually doing it. This often causes him to spend too much time ambitiously planning out his projects. There have been multiple instances where he has done this in various past high school projects. Creative


Because of how extra he can be, Tristan has a gift for generating a lot of imaginative as well as really dumb ideas. Ever since he was young he had always had a obession with building and creating. He once cut a katana out of wood on his own and carved his own chess pieces, etc.

Easy-Going
